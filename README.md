## 📞 Task 1: Call Center Performance Analysis
**Objective:** Provide the Call Center Manager with a KPI dashboard to monitor agent performance and customer satisfaction.

### 💡 Key Insights
*   **Efficiency Gap:** Out of 5,000 calls, there is an **18.92% missed call rate**, indicating a need for optimized staffing during peak periods.
*   **Resolution Challenges:** **27.08% of issues remain unresolved**, which correlates with the below-optimal customer satisfaction score of **3.40/5.00**.
*   **Volume Drivers:** Inquiries are concentrated in **Streaming, Tech Support, and Payments**, suggesting these areas are ripe for AI Chatbot automation.

### 🚀 Recommendations
*   **Staffing Optimization:** Align agent shifts with high-volume periods to lower the missed call rate.
*   **Self-Service FAQ:** Develop automated responses for common "Streaming" and "Payment" issues to reduce agent load.

## 📉 Task 2: Customer Retention (Churn) Analysis
**Objective:** Identify high-risk segments and recommend proactive strategies to reduce customer attrition.

### 💡 Key Insights
*   **Contract Risk:** Month-to-month customers represent **88.55% of total churn**, particularly those with **tenure < 1 year (55.48%)**.
*   **Payment Friction:** Customers using **Electronic Checks** churn at a significantly higher rate (**57.3%**) than those on automated billing.
*   **Service Impact:** Fiber optic users show a **41.89% churn rate**, potentially indicating service quality issues compared to other offerings.

### 🚀 Recommendations
*   **Migration Incentives:** Offer discounts for customers moving from month-to-month to annual contracts.
*   **Bundling Strategy:** Introduce "Online Security" or "Tech Support" trials for new users to increase service "stickiness."

## 🤝 Task 3: Diversity & Inclusion (HR Analytics)
**Objective:** Visualize gender balance and promotion trends at the executive management level.

### 💡 Key Insights
*   **The Glass Ceiling:** Female representation drops sharply at the **Director and Executive levels**, despite balanced hiring at entry levels.
*   **Promotion Bottleneck:** While performance ratings show no gender bias, men are promoted at higher rates into senior leadership roles.
*   **Retention Risk:** Female turnover is higher in senior positions, leading to a "leaky pipeline" for future executive roles.

### 🚀 Recommendations
*   **Mentorship & Sponsorship:** Establish a formal sponsorship program for female managers to bridge the gap to leadership.
*   **Bias Training:** Implement standardized promotion criteria to ensure high performance ratings translate equally into advancement for all genders.
